# End of Life coming soon

I really love this game. I have been playing it to keep my old Samsung S7 Edge alive and going. However, lately (starting January 14th 2021) the device is really breaking down. The phone was already severely damaged (except for the screen and some of the buttons) but it is getting hard to use now. The big problem now is that the power button is getting harder and harder to press (other than the screen not being broken, the volume up button is completely broken, all the ports are broken, the speakers are heavily damaged, the SIM card slot is damaged, the SD card slot might be damaged, the battery is failing, and the device doesn't have much memory) It is leaving creases in my fingers from playing like this. I don't know how much longer I will be able to play this game before I have to retire my old phone and quit playing. An end of life date may come unannounced.
